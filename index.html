WHY 5G FOR BUSINESS

BETTER CONNECTIONS IN BUSY PLACES

Sometimes in busy places connections can slow down, or not work at all. And when too many people try to connect in one place at one time, networks reach their limits. With 5GEE, our network gives customers a more reliable connection in busy places like train stations and stadiums. So they can video call, stream and download in the busiest places.

EVEN FASTER AND BETTER QUALITY

With 5G speeds, you can now download and transfer large, high quality files in seconds rather than minutes, stream in the highest resolution and enjoy perfect video calls.

INSTANT CONNECTION

5G brings almost instant internet connection, making your daily tasks feel super quick. Many of the exciting things we’ll use 5G for are still being created. Think amazing new augmented and virtual reality experiences. It will change everything, eventually.

5G FLEXIBLE PLANS

Your needs are always changing. Our 5G plans are flexible so you can give them what they need, when they need it. Benefits like roaming, extra data and international calls can be swapped around as and when they’re needed.

5G EXCLUSIVES

You need to be able to communicate securely – and at any time. With MobileIron Threat Defense, you’ll get a pop-up message to warn about any potential cyber threats. Further protecting your key business tools. You won’t have to worry about running out of data either.

With Data Reserve, we’ll switch you over to 4G rather than switching them off if their monthly allowance runs out. So you can still stay connected to your customers. If you need to do more, faster, you can get a 5G data pass until your allowance kicks in again.
